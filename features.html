We have incorporated many features into our apple watch app

SOS

Call: Call 911 for any emergency

Text: You can decide if you would like to send a text to someone regarding a sitation.

Alert: Alert sent to 911 and emergency contact of you being in trouble and need help!

Beam

This feature will send your current location to your emergency contacts!

Request a Ride

Call an uber to get to your destination safely through this feature. You can enter your location through a microphone or select it on the map.

Fast Alert

This feature will alert the surrounding area and the people around you with a loud sound. All you have to do in hold a button for 10 seconds!
